# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Advertisers, operators, and analysts who upload CSV or Excel exports, choose fields, compare campaign or ad group metrics, and inspect the raw table when something needs checking.

## Product Purpose

This tool turns ad performance spreadsheets into readable charts and searchable tables. Success means users can keep every useful row, select dimensions and metrics quickly, and understand the chart without fighting layout or controls.

## Brand Personality

Clear, practical, focused. The interface should feel like a reliable workbench for repeated analysis, not a marketing page.

## Anti-references

Avoid misaligned form controls, cramped chart labels, hidden chart legends, decorative UI, and inconsistent input styles that make the tool feel unfinished.

## Design Principles

- Keep controls aligned and predictable.
- Preserve data fidelity before visual simplification.
- Keep key context visible while users scroll large charts.
- Use restrained styling that supports scanning and repeated work.
- Make table and filter workflows feel deliberate, not bolted on.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, visible focus states, native form semantics where practical, and layouts that remain usable with dense Chinese labels and long metric names.
